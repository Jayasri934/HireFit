About HireFit

HireFit is an AI-powered resume analysis tool designed to help job seekers optimize their resumes and discover their best-fit career paths.

Key Features:

  • **Job Match Score:** Uses **TF-IDF Vectorization** and **Cosine Similarity** to instantly grade your resume against any specific job description.
  • **Skill Analysis:** Extracts and analyzes skills from your resume using a **keyword lookup dictionary** for targeted career guidance.
  • **Interactive UI:** Modern dark theme with immediate visual feedback, including **Progress Bars** and **Color-Coded Suggestions**.
  • **Secure Profile:** User authentication is managed by **Flask-Login** and passwords are securely hashed using **Werkzeug**.
